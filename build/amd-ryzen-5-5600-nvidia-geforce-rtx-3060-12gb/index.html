FAQ

Is AMD Ryzen 5 5600 good enough for NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 12GB?

Yes — at 1080p, the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 12GB is the limiter, so the AMD Ryzen 5 5600 has headroom. This is a balanced pairing for this resolution.

What FPS can I expect with AMD Ryzen 5 5600 and NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 12GB?

Our model estimates roughly 105 FPS average and 76 FPS 1% lows at 1080p in AAA gaming with default settings. Actual results vary by game.

Should I upgrade the CPU or GPU in this build?

Upgrade the GPU first. The graphics card is the bottleneck, which is expected at 1080p.
